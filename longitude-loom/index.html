HOW THE CLOTH KEEPS TIME

No row is revised.

Place names seed the colors and the quiet irregularity of the thread. Every crossing is deterministic. A knot begins influencing the next row and slowly releases its pull over later journeys, but the cloth already woven is never recalculated. Each route is kept only in this browser, ready when you return.
